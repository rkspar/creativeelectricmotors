Welcome to Wixel Design

Let's Start

The Impact

Just know that each WIXEL purchase will go a long way! If you're a rebel and chose not to view our video below , here's why a WIXEL purchase makes a major impact

Driggs, the CEO and Founder of WIXEL, personally owns an emotional support dog and knows how impactful her Chocolate lab has been for her depression. A percentage of WIXEL's sales goes directly to help fund service dogs for our Veterans, FOR FREE, to assist them with similar mental and physical disabilities. Driggs is also a California girl at heart and protecting our oceans is numero uno priority. We all want healthy fishies and cute doggies running around; so, impact your wall with a WIXEL and you'll be able to impact the world!

Risks & Challenges

Our main challenge is being able to fund this project upfront with our manufacturer, not to mention actually hitting out goal! Buying 50 at a time costs major per unit, BUT ordering quantities of 500 at a time costs a significant amount up front as well. Our main challenge has been raising enough funds to order 500 ahead of time and have them ready to sell directly to consumers online.

Wixel Projects

Each WIXEL is made of Birch plywood, approximately 5/8" thick. If you're lucky, you may wind up with a knot or two in your design, which only adds to the aesthetic appeal! You'll also find our engraved logo on the back. Lastly, there is a hole in the back of each WIXEL for you to hang these on your wall.
